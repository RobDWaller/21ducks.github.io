Membership: Nick Nelms

Nick has supported Aylesbury United from an early age and is dedicated to inspiring a new generation of fans while working towards bringing the club back home. As a Senior Employee Benefit Consultant, Nick offers professional expertise in communications, benefits strategy and organisational support. Skills that are invaluable in helping the club and its community grow and thrive. He also manages 21CD's Gold Club, further strengthening the connection between supporters and the club.

Website & Socials: Rob Waller

Rob is a dedicated supporter of Aylesbury United, having adopted the club after moving to Aylesbury in 2019. He is passionate about seeing the Ducks return home to Aylesbury and is committed to building a strong, supporter-owned club. Rob is a Director of Software Engineering, and he brings a wealth of technological expertise to the table, which he actively employs to help the Club and Trust grow and thrive. He manages the Trust's website and social media accounts, ensuring the club maintains a strong online presence.
