Chairman: Andy Martin

Andy Martin is chairman of 21 Ducks the Aylesbury United Supporters' Trust and is our main representative on the Aylesbury United club board. Andy has supported the club ever since he saw The Ducks beat Oxford City 4-1 at his first ever match in late 1997, he was hooked from that first game. Part of the attraction was the passionate fans and their ability to make some noise. As a result he can often be heard singing and shouting in the terraces! Andy works for NHS England as a Programme Manager and brings a wealth of experience in managing teams and organsing initiatives, which he uses to help our Trust and Club move forward. He is determined to help our community club return home! #BringTheDucksHome Andy once scored a goal for Aylesbury United Supporters Team (The Chicken Run) and this was announced over the tannoy at The Ducks game that afternoon although the goal was accredited to 'Andy Marshall'. Hopefully you won't hear him repeat similar mistakes over the tannoy given he is the man behind the microphone at every home game.

Vice-Chairman: Mike Snell

Mike has been a loyal supporter of Aylesbury United since moving to the town in 1995. From 2015 until 2025, he served as the club's official match photographer, capturing countless memories before retiring from the role. He remains closely involved, currently working on a detailed history of the club covering the years 1897 to 1955 due for publication later this year. A former journalist, writer and corporate communications specialist, Mike retired in 2013. He continues to put his experience to good use as a Trustee of the Cancer Care and Haematology Fund, supporting vital treatment services within the Buckinghamshire Healthcare NHS Trust. Passionate about the future of Aylesbury United, Mike is committed to helping the club finally return home to the town. In the early 1970s, Mike used to race and rally cars - until the money ran out!

Secretary: Sheila Cotton

A lifelong supporter of Aylesbury United, Sheila has been following the Ducks for over 70 years initially through her father and with growing passion since her son Lee became a dedicated fan over 35 years ago. Now, she's proudly encouraging the third generation of her family, her grandchildren, to carry on the tradition. Sheila brings a wealth of professional experience from her time as an NHS Business Manager and Director's Secretary, supporting the Trust with a calm head and a sharp eye for detail. Her hopes for the Club? To see Aylesbury United back playing in Aylesbury and to build a consistent team rooted in local talent.

Membership: Nick Nelms

Nick has supported Aylesbury United from an early age and is dedicated to inspiring a new generation of fans while working towards bringing the club back home. As a Senior Employee Benefit Consultant, Nick offers professional expertise in communications, benefits strategy and organisational support. Skills that are invaluable in helping the club and its community grow and thrive. He also manages 21CD's Gold Club, further strengthening the connection between supporters and the club.

Website & Socials: Rob Waller

Rob is a dedicated supporter of Aylesbury United, having adopted the club after moving to Aylesbury in 2019. He is passionate about seeing the Ducks return home to Aylesbury and is committed to building a strong, supporter-owned club. Rob is a Director of Software Engineering, and he brings a wealth of technological expertise to the table, which he actively employs to help the Club and Trust grow and thrive. He manages the Trust's website and social media accounts, ensuring the club maintains a strong online presence.
