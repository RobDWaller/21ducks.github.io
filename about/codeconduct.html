Let's Keep Aylesbury United FC a Club We Can All Be Proud Of

As supporters, we all want to see the team do well. But being part of Aylesbury United isn't just about results, it's about how we carry ourselves as a fanbase and what we stand for.

That's why we're sharing a quick reminder about the AUFC Code of Conduct. It sets the standard for behaviour across the club, including players, coaches, officials and supporters.

Why It Matters

We all get passionate on matchdays. That's what makes football what it is. But there's a line and it's important we stay on the right side of it. As 21st Century Ducks, we want to support the team in a way that reflects the values of the club and the community behind it.

The Code is about creating a safe, respectful environment for everyone involved, whether that's on the pitch, in the dugout or in the stands.

For Supporters:

  • Back the team positively - home or away
  • Keep it respectful towards officials, opposition players and fans
  • Don't tolerate discrimination, abuse or aggression - call it out if you see it

For Everyone:

  • Act with integrity and fairness
  • Respect the decisions of match officials
  • Represent the club in a way that does it justice

This Is Our Club Too

We're proud of Aylesbury United and proud to stand behind the team. But how we show our support matters. The atmosphere we create has a real impact on the players, the staff and everyone watching.

So let's keep the standards high. Let's be loud, be united and be the kind of supporters others want to stand alongside.
