Get in on the Action: Join the 50:50 Draw!

Want to support Aylesbury United and have a chance to win real cash every month? The 50:50 Draw is a fun and easy way to do both and the more members who join, the bigger the prizes!

What Is It?

It's simple: Every month, each member who enters gets a ticket (or more!) for just £5 per ticket. Every ticket has a unique number… and if your number is drawn, you win!

It's called the 50:50 Draw because 50% of all money raised goes straight into prizes the rest helps support the Club through the Supporters' Trust.

What Can You Win?

Each month, there are two guaranteed prizes up for grabs:

1st Prize: £100

2nd Prize: £25

But here's the exciting part: The more people enter, the bigger the prize pool grows! All winnings scale with participation and payouts are reviewed regularly to reflect growing membership.

How Much Does It Cost?

Just £5 per ticket per month — that's only £1.25 a week for a shot at winning and a great way to give back.

You can pay in two easy ways:

  • Monthly Standing Order from your bank
  • Annual Cheque Payment of £60 per ticket for a whole year of entries

Signing up is quick and you'll be automatically entered into every draw. Whether you win or not, you're helping bring the club closer to home and keeping the spirit of Aylesbury United alive.

Ready to take your shot? Join the draw. Back the club. Win big.
