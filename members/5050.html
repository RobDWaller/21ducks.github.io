What is the 50:50 draw?

The 50:50 draw is a membership scheme whereby each member purchases a ticket for a small fee each month. Each member will be allocated their own unique number for each ticket they purchase.

A draw will be made every month, with the owner of the drawn number winning the prize!

What can I win?

50:50 draw minimum prizes are as follows:

1st Prize: £100

2nd Prize: £25

However, the size of the prize is dictated by how many people join the draw.

Under the rules for the Draw, the Supporters Trust are commited to pay out 50% of the income in prizes. Amounts payable will be reviewed on an ongoing basis as membership levels vary.

How much does it cost?

The scheme costs just £5 per month to join (£1.25 per week), payable either as a monthly Standing Order from member's bank accounts, or by cheque for £60 per ticket purchased covering 12 months.
