The Gold Club is Aylesbury United's super season ticket!

We're excited to announce some important changes regarding the management of the Gold Club. Starting this season, the 21st Century Ducks Supporters Club will be taking over the administration and management of the Gold Club.

The proceeds from our Gold Club membership directly support the playing budget and have played a crucial role in helping the team remain competitive at Step 4.

For a minimum membership fee of £25 a month, it includes these benefits:

  • Free entry to all home 2025-26 League matches
  • Membership card can be used by a friend in your absence
  • Free entry for one guest at three League games per season (with prior notice)
  • Access to the boardroom, pre-match and at half-time, with free tea and coffee
  • 3 pre-match meals, with Q&A with the manager, a board update, guest speakers, quizzes, and a chance to talk 'duck' with fellow members
  • Exclusive WhatsApp group with up-to-the-minute updates of important Ducks news
  • Your name included in the matchday programme, and on the club website
  • Free matchday programme advert for business members.

All of this for a minimum fee of £25 a month by standing order.
